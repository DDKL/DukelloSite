About Me


Born in Seoul, South Korea, Duke moved to the United States just before his 7th birthday, leaving all of his relatives behind in search for opportunities. He became the top student in many of his classes. He excelled in language arts, mathematics, athletics, and more. He has been playing the piano for over 15 years. He's known for his innate talent at playing pretty much anything by ear.
